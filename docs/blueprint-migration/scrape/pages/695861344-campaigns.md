Campaign names and descriptions

Automated marketing emails are sent daily and target patients for the following campaigns:

Name

Description

Birthdays #1 - Email and 7 days print

An email will go out to active patients on the date of their birthday. Print letters will be generated 7 days before the patient's birthday.

Birthdays #2 - Email and 60 days print

An email will go out to active patients on the date of their birthday. Print letters will be generated 60 days before the patient's birthday.

Cancel and no show #1 - 1 week

Active patients whose last appointment was cancelled or no-show, and 7 days before the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn't have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Cancel and no show #2 - 2 weeks

Active patients whose last appointment was cancelled or no-show, and 14 days before the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn't have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Cancel and no show #3 - 30 days

Active patients whose last appointment was cancelled or no-show, and 30 days before the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn't have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Clean and check

A clean and check email or print letter will be generated for active patients with 6-month intervals (6, 12, 18, etc.) from the date of fitting as long as the hearing aid is covered under warranty. Also makes sure the patient doesn&rsquo;t have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Google Review Request #1 - 1 day

Active patients and QuickAdds who have an appointment in completed status from the prior day. This event type must have online review enabled. Makes sure the patient has not chosen to abstain from online review.

Hearing aid 1 year old

Active patients whose active hearing aid(s) have been purchased 1 year prior to the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn&rsquo;t have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Hearing aid 2 years old

Active patients whose active hearing aid(s) have been purchased 2 years prior to the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn&rsquo;t have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Hearing aid 3 years old

Active patients whose active hearing aid(s) have been purchased 3 years prior to the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn&rsquo;t have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

Hearing aid 4 years old

Hearing aid 5 years old

Active patients whose active hearing aid(s) have been purchased 5 years prior to the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn&rsquo;t have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

New patient welcome message

Active patients and QuickAdds who have been created in the system on the current date and that have an appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

New QuickAdd Contact Inquiry

Active QuickAdds who have been created in the system from the prior day and have no upcoming appointment. 

Not tested in a year

Active patients whose last audiology (test date) entry is one year prior to the current date. Also makes sure the patient doesn&rsquo;t have an active appointment scheduled within the next 30 days.

 Patient Referral #1 - 1 day

Active patients and QuickAdds who have been created in the system from the prior day and have an existing patient listed as their referral source.

Post fitting #1 - 1 week

Active patients whose latest fit hearing aid fitting is 1 week from the current date.

Post fitting #2 - 2 weeks

Active patients whose latest fit hearing aid fitting is 2 weeks from the current date.
