Selecting aids to loan

 - On the Patient browser tab, open the patient&rsquo;s file.

 - Click the Hearing aids tab. 

 - On the Hearing aids tab, click the Add aid button.

 - Select Loan from stock from the drop-down menu.

 - From the Select stock aid(s) dialog box, select the Location.

 - Click on a hearing aid from the inventory list. Drag-and-drop the aid to the Left or Right side. 

Drag-and-drop a second aid to the other side in order to loan two hearing aids at once.
 - Click Add. 

 - From the Enter recall date dialog box, click on the calendar icon to select a date from the calendar.

 - Click Ok.

Manage recalls in the recall module. See .

Viewing aids on loan

View aids with On loan status in three different areas within Blueprint:

 - From the patient Hearing aids tab, view an On loan aid under the Ordered/delivered items tab.

 - From the Inventory drop-down menu, select Manage Inventory to view an On loan aid, along with the name of the patient and recall date. 

 - From the Recalls module, utilize the Recall type drop-down menu, and select Loaned aid due to see a listing of patient names and recall dates.
