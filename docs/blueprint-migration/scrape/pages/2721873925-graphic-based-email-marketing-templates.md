Overview

Blueprint Solution's enhanced email editor puts the power of customization in your hands. With graphic-based email marketing templates, you can create captivating designs that will grab your audience's attention from the moment they open your email. Whether you want to personalize headers and footers, seamlessly add images and buttons, or shape the layout to match your vision, the possibilities are endless.

Managing email headers and footers

Email headers and footers can be designed for branding purposes and to provide information about your company, display a logo, link to a website, and include custom translations. 

Access custom headers and footers for your email templates by navigating to Setup > Templates > Email headers and footers. 

From this screen, you can do the following:  

 - Use QuickFind search to easily locate a template.

 - Select a Template type from the drop-down menu to filter by headers or footers.

 - Select a Language from the drop-down menu to filter templates by language.

 - Create new, Edit, Duplicate, Preview, or Deactivate email headers and footers 

 - You can also edit header/footer translations and launch the template editor. 

Viewing marketing email templates

Access the list of marketing email templates by navigating to Setup > Templates > Marketing > Email.

Gain visibility into the following key components:

 - Sending email address: This is the sending email address for all marketing email campaigns. By changing this email address, you will also be changing the email address for marketing automation.

 - Quick Find Search: This tool can be used to easily find the campaign you're looking for

 - Language drop-down menu: This tool allows you to filter campaigns by translation

The table of templates includes:

 - Template name

 - Template translations

 - Header and footer templates

 - Details of the template's most recent modification (date, time, and user)

Creating a new marketing email template

Navigate to Setup > Marketing > Email and select Create new. Then, follow these steps:

 - Give the template a unique name 

Optionally,

 - Select the header template. The selected header will be added to the top of the email 

 - Select the footer template. The selected footer will be added to the bottom of the email

 - Click Save

Editing translations

Defaulting to your system's language, the editor offers extensive translation editing capabilities. 

To add a new language, R-click the email template > edit template translations > select the paper + pencil icon to modify the translation and/or subject line. 

Use the drop-down menu to introduce a new language template.

Launching the template editor

Edit your email headers, footers, and templates by Launching the template editor. Activate the template editor by r-clicking the template > launch template editor. 

This action opens the Online Email Editor in a new browser window. Unleash your creativity and customize the email template with the following comprehensive set of features:

 - Columns

 - Headings

 - Text

 - Images
