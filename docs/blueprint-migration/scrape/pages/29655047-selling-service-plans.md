To sell the patient a service plan and add it to their file:

 - Sell the patient the service. For step-by-step instructions on how to sell a service, click here. - If you do not see the service there, you may need to create the service.  For instructions on how to do so, click here.

 - Add the service plan to the patient file using the instructions below:

Adding service plans to patient files

 - Navigate to the patient's Hearing aids tab.
 - Right-click on the aid or manufacturer item that the service plan will be added to.
 - Select Edit details.

 - Select the Service plan from the drop down menu. - Optionally, adjust the  Service plan expiration date by typing in the date or by using the red calendar icon.

 - Click Ok.
