Viewing patient groups

 - Click the Marketing button on the main toolbar.

 - Click the Patient groups tab.

 - This will open a list of your patient groups, where you will find the following information:

 -  Description of the group

 - Number of Members in a group

 - If a group note is required for a specific patient group

Patient groups can be created and used to include specific patients in marketing campaigns.

Creating patient groups

 - Click the Marketing button on the main toolbar.

 - Click the Patient groups tab.

 - On the Patient groups tab, click Create new.

 - In the Create patient group dialog box, enter a group name and click Create.

 - Optionally, check the Group note required checkbox. If enabled, a group note will be required when adding a patient to this group. An example of a required note could be a membership number for a rewards program. 

Editing patient groups

 - Click the Marketing button on the main toolbar

 - Click the Patient groups tab

 - On the Patient groups tab, select a patient group and click Edit details

 - In the Edit patient group dialog box, you can make the following adjustments:

 - Modify the group name

 - Modify if the group note is required or not required

5. Click Update.

Deactivating patient groups

 - Click the Marketing button on the main toolbar.

 - Click the Patient groups tab.

 - On the Patient groups tab, right-click the desired patient group and choose 'Edit details, or select the desired group and choose Edit details button at the bottom of the screen.

 - Uncheck the &lsquo;Active&rsquo; box.

 - Select Update.

notePatients will remain in the group that is deactivated, as these can be reactivated at a later time

Patients will remain in the group that is deactivated, as these can be reactivated at a later time

Reactivating deactivated patient groups

 - Click the Marketing button on the main toolbar.

 - Click the Patient groups tab.

 - On the Patient groups tab, uncheck the &lsquo;Show active items only&rsquo; box.

 - Right-click the desired patient group and choose 'Edit details, or select the desired group and choose Edit details button at the bottom of the screen. 

 - Check the &lsquo;Active&rsquo; box.

 - Select Update.

Viewing members in patient groups

 - Click the Marketing button on the main toolbar.

 - Click the Patient groups tab.
