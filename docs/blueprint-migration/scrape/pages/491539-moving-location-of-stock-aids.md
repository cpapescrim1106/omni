Selecting stock aids to move

 - Click the Inventory drop-down menu, and select Manage Inventory.
 - On the Inventory tab, click on a location listed. If operating in multiple clinic locations, select <All> to see all inventory aids.

Locate the desired aid, searching by serial number in the Serial number field or by aid status clicking on the Status drop-down menu.
 - Click on a hearing aid with 'In stock' status.

Moving stock aids

 - With an 'In stock' hearing aid selected, drag-and-drop the aid to another location listed.

 - In the Move stock aid(s) dialog box, enter an optional note and click OK. 

 - In the Move stock aid(s) confirmation dialog box, click OK. 

The status of the aid shows as 'Sent to...' the new location.

Acknowledging the receipt of stock aids

 - On the Inventory tab, click on a location listed. If operating in multiple clinic locations, select <All> to see all inventory aids.
 - Click on an aid with 'Arriving from...' status. 

 - Right-click the aid and select Acknowledge receipt. 
 - The aid will be placed 'In stock' at the new location.
