Selecting loaned aids

 - On the Patient browser tab, open the patient&rsquo;s file.

 - Click the Hearing aids tab.

 - Click on a hearing aid with On loan status.

To select multiple loaned aids, press and hold the Control key (Ctrl) on the keyboard while clicking on the aids.

Generating loaner agreements

 - Right-click the On loan aid and select Loaner agreement.  

 - An agreement will automatically populate within Blueprint. 

Use the default Loaner agreement within Blueprint OMS, or upload your clinic-specific agreement your system from the Setup > Templates > Forms menu.

Signing loaner agreements electronically

Documents enabled for electronic signing can be signed using a tablet or a signature pad.

Signing using a tablet

To use a tablet for electronic signatures, the online forms feature needs to be enabled and a tablet needs to be set up at the patient's assigned location.

For more information, see: Setting up online forms and Setting up and using tablets.

 - Click Sign on tablet.

 - The Sign on tablet dialog box will appear. Select a tablet to send the loaner agreement to, and set the title and category to archive the loaner agreement under. Then click Ok.

 - On the tablet, enter the patient's date of birth.

 - The loaner agreement will appear on the tablet. Click into the rectangle that says Sign here.

 - Have the patient enter their signature and hit CreateBlue. Then tap SubmitGreen on the form.

Then, the signed loaner agreement will appear in the patient's Documents tab.

Signing using a signature pad

US-based customers can order signature pads here: Signature Pad Order Form.

 - Click Sign document.

 - As the patient signs the signature pad, the signature will appear within the Sign document dialog box.

 - When the patient has finished signing the signature pad, click Done.

Archiving loaner agreements Archiving loaner agreements

 - Click the Archive as PDF button to archive the agreement under the patient Documents tab.

 - Within the Add document dialog box, set a Title, a Category, and optionally a Description and a Status.

 - Click Save.

Extracting the loaner agreement out of Blueprint OMS

There are two ways to extract the loaner agreement out of Blueprint OMS.

 - Click the Save icon in the top left-hand corner to save the agreement to your computer.

 - Click the Print icon in the top left-hand corner to print the agreement.
