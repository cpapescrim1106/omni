Receiving patient deposits Receiving patient deposits

A deposit can be taken in order to receive payment from a patient, without applying the payment against an open invoice. A deposit can be  against an open invoice at a later time.

 - To receive a deposit, do one of the following: - On the Patient browser tab, open the patient's file.
 - On the Patient browser tab, click on the desired patient.

 - Click the Patient drop-down menu, and select Receive deposit.

 - In the Receive deposit dialog box, enter the Amount received, Payment method, and Check #, if Check is selected as the payment method.
 - Click Save.

QuickBooksThe deposit appears as an unapplied payment in the patient's Sales history tab and is recorded in QuickBooks. In the patient's Sales history tab, right-click an open invoice and select Receive/apply payments to apply the deposit. The deposit will be applied against the corresponding invoice in QuickBooks.

When a deposit is received from a patient, an entry automatically appears in that patient's journal to indicate that the deposit was received.
