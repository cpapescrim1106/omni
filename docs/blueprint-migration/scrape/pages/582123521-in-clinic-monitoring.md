Overview

The in-clinic monitor lets you see who is currently in your clinic in real time. When a patient arrives, you can mark them as Arrived so they can begin filling out paperwork. Once their paperwork is complete, mark them as Ready. If no paperwork is required, you can select Arrived & Ready in one step. The system will automatically track how early or late the patient arrived. Under the Patient Summary tab, you can view their visit statistics.

When a patient is marked as Arrived or Ready, a notification is sent to the provider&rsquo;s computer. The provider can respond with preset messages such as On my way, Need 5 minutes, Running behind, or they can send a custom message. Responses are sent back to the front office.

Once the provider begins the appointment, right-click the appointment and select In Progress. This resets the timer and tracks the appointment&rsquo;s duration.

When the appointment is finished and the patient returns to the front office, right-click the appointment and select Completed. This updates the patient&rsquo;s status and enables you to run the Appointment Analysis report for appointment statistics.
