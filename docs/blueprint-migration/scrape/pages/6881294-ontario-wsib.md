Billing WSIB electronically (Canada only) Billing WSIB electronically (Canada only)

Canadian clients can optionally bill WSIB electronically, through Telus, directly in Blueprint OMS. Please contact Blueprint Solutions for additional information on setting up electronic claim submission.

Claims are submitted nightly. Updated claim status and EFT payments are received from WSIB every Monday and Thursday morning.

Note: Telus requires periodic password updates. Be sure to forward updated passwords to the Blueprint Solutions technical team to ensure that electronic billing continues to function properly.
