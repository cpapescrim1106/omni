Creating service plans

 - Navigate to the Setup menu and click on Service plans.

 - Click Create new.

 - After adding the Name and Plan length, click Create.

Editing service plans

 - Navigate to the Setup menu and select Service plans.
 - Highlight the plan that changes will be made to and select Edit details.

 - Make the necessary changes and then click Update.
