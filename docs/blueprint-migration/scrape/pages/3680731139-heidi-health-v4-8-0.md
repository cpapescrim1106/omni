We are thrilled to announce a powerful new integration with Heidi Health, a leading platform for advanced AI-driven medical scribing. This innovative tool is designed to streamline clinical documentation by transcribing patient visits, generating clinical notes, completing forms, and dictating letters&mdash;all in real-time. By leveraging Heidi Health, clinicians can spend less time on administrative tasks and more time focusing on patient care.

Once configured, Blueprint users will have seamless access to their Heidi Health subscription directly within the Blueprint interface, allowing them to effortlessly create patient journal entries and enhance workflow efficiency.

We are confident that this integration will help our users improve productivity and deliver a higher standard of care.
