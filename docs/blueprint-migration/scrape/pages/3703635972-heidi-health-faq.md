Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can multiple Blueprint OMS users use the integration with the same Heidi Health account?

A: No, each Heidi Health account can only be linked to one Blueprint user account.

 

Q: What Heidi Health plan do I need?

A: Each user will be integrated with their own Heidi Health account and must have the Practice Plan. New users will get a 30-day free trial of the Practice Plan.

Q: Is there anything different about Blueprint OMS journal entries that came from a Heidi Health session?

A: No. Once a Heidi Health session pushes a note to Blueprint OMS, the created journal entry in Blueprint OMS is a normal journal entry. It is not directly linked to anything outside of Blueprint OMS and cannot be affected by any further interactions with Heidi Health or the integration.

 

Q: What should the user do if they closed the Heidi Health session before the journal entry was pushed?

A: The user can start another Heidi Health session for that patient from Blueprint OMS, and it will load the most recent session for that patient within the last 12 hours. The session can also be accessed within the Heidi Health user portal.

Have questions about Heidi Health? Click here for Heidi Health articles. Contact Heidi Health here.```
