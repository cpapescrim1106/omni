Downloading Instructions

First, add the subscription to your Google Calendar account:

 - You can find information regarding the iCal URL . 

 - Log into your Gmail account, and access your Google calendar.

 - In Google Calendar, click the drop-down menu next to Other Calendars, and click Add by URL.
 - Click Add Calendar.
 - Google will begin to synchronize the calendar, and it will appear on the screen.
 - Optionally, click the drop-down menu next to the calendar listed under Other Calendars, and click Calendar Settings.
 - Change the Calendar Name (e.g. Steven McDonald), and click Save.
Second, verify that the new calendar is synchronized to your device:

 - Open the Calendar app on your phone/tablet.
 - Click the menu button, click Calendars to display, and do one of the following: - Click Calendars to sync, check the box next to the newly added calendar and click Ok.
 - Click Calendars, click Display, check the box next to the newly added calendar and click Done.

 - View the calendar to see appointments.
