This update introduces an innovative AI-powered beta feature designed to significantly enhance the patient journal note-taking process. By integrating advanced artificial intelligence, this update simplifies documentation, increases accuracy, and saves valuable time for audiologists and clinic staff. With the addition of speech-to-text functionality, clinicians can dictate their observations, and the AI will transcribe and structure the notes. This seamless integration reduces the need for typing and speeds up the note-taking process during and after patient visits. This new feature is designed to streamline your practice&rsquo;s documentation process, improve the quality of patient records, and ultimately enhance patient care.

This feature is accessible in many locations throughout the software.

Location

Global

In the bottom right-hand corner of Blueprint OMS, next to chat.
