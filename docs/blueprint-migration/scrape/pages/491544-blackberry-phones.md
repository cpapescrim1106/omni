Downloading instructions

First, to sync with Blackberry, download Google Sync and install it on the phone.

 - Log into your Gmail account, and access your Google calendar.
 - In Google Calendar, click the drop-down menu next to Other Calendars, and click Add by URL.
 - Enter the unique iCal URL. You can find more information on the iCal URL . 

 - Click Add Calendar.
 - Google will begin to sync your calendar, and it will appear on the screen.
 - Optionally, click the drop-down menu next to the calendar listed under Other Calendars, and click Calendar Settings.
 - Change the Calendar Name (e.g. Steven McDonald), and click Save.
 - Your phone should automatically be set to sync with your Google calendar.
Second, verify that the new calendar is synchronized to your device:

 - Click on Calendar.
 - Click on Settings. 
 - Click on Calendar Sync. You should see your Google account already listed.
 - Ensure Auto-Sync is checked.
